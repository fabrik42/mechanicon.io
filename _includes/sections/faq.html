faq

Can I bring my keyboards?

Yes, please! This is what Mechanicon is all about. We will have enough tables so that everyone can bring their keyboards for display!

Can I come even if I don't have a keyboard yet?

Of course! We would love to show you around! Mechanicon is the perfect chance to get a deep dive into this hobby.

I want to bring a lot of keyboards and I don't want to carry them from my parking lot.

Contact us, we can arrange some help for unloading and watching for your stuff! Do not just park in front of the building, this will get you in trouble.

I want a fancy sign next to my keyboard.

We got you covered! You will find signs and pens at the event! If you want to print your own signs at home, use the original template.

Where should I go if I get hungry?

The location 2026 is in Bornheim, a popular district of Frankfurt am Main. The heart of Bornheim is the Berger Strasse — with a variety of dining options including restaurants, cafés, fast food outlets, and supermarkets.

You collect donations. What if you collect more than needed?

We don't want to make any money out of this event. In case we collect more donations than we need for this year, we will keep it for the next Mechanicon. If we don't spend the money within 12 months, we will donate the money to a local non-profit.

I have even more questions!

We are happy to help! Just send the organizers a message: hello@mechanicon.io.
