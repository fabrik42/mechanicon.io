Welcome to Bornheim!

Saalbau Bornheim

Just as in the last years, the Mechanicon takes place in the Saalbau Bornheim in Frankfurt am Main. It is a huge venue, with lots of space and tables for you, your keyboards and everyone else!

address
Arnsburger Str. 24, 60385 Frankfurt am Main
access
We recommend public transport. Take the U4 from the main train station to get there.
parking
There is a parking garage in the same building (approach via Arnsburger Strasse). Space is limited. Do NOT park in the backyard — you will get towed!
→ Open in Google Maps

⮐ There are many vendors in the foyer, also a bar with softdrinks, snacks and our registration desk (go there first, to check in). Next to it, you will find the huge ballroom with the keyboard exhibition. Upstairs: a dedicated room for talks and workshops!
